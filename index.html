    The Analystics Edge.

    This is a analytics course that covered multiple machine learning topics. The course was offered by MIT through Edx platform. Topics ranged from linear and logistic regression, to decision trees and cluster analysis, to Random Forrest and Support Vector Machine. The course final project was developed by kaggle.
